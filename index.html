Welcome to the Haukai Restaurant!

The Haukai Restaurant is based in the Bay of Islands and offers a wide range of foods, specialising in Maori food. The dinner menu features both Maori and Pakeha food and drink options. Along with the fantastic food, we also offer a first-hand experience with te ao Maori as we use karakia for blessing the food.
